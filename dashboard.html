Nyondo stock system

Nyondo stock is a stock management system designed to help businesses record, track, and manage their products efficiently.
Thee system helps store owners know the quantity of goods available, products sold, and items that need restocking.

Purpose of the project

The purpose of the Nyondo stock system is to simplify inventory management and reduce errors when tracking goods in a business.
It helps business keep accurate records of their stock.


Features of the system

  • Add new products to the stock
  • View available stock
  • Update product quantities
  • Record sold items
  • Alert when stock is low

Benefits of the system

  1. Saves time when managing stock
  2. Reduces mistakes when counting items
  3. Helps business know when to restock
  4. Improves organization of products

Target Users

The system is designed for shop owners, Supermarkets, and store managers who need easy way to manage their inventory.
